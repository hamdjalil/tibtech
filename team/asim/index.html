Asim Iqbal

About

Dr. Asim Iqbal is the Co-founder & CEO of Tibbling Technologies, where he leads the Artificial Intelligence division. With a Master's and Ph.D. in Machine Learning and Computational Neuroscience from the Univeristy & ETH Zürich, Dr. Iqbal brings extensive expertise in developing innovative Multimodal AI systems for healthcare applications.

Dr. Iqbal also holds the position of Adjunct Assistant Professor at Weill Cornell Medicine in New York and has previously worked as a Machine Learning Scientist at Google X, Cajal Neuroscience, EPFL, and IBM Research. At Tibbling Technologies, Dr. Iqbal leads a team of Ph.D. scientists and engineers, collaborating with renowned institutions like the Broad Institute and Harvard Medical School to develop cutting-edge AI products for healthcare applications. His research work is published in top AI venues, including Nature Machine Intelligence and CVPR.
